Who We Are

Company
Overview

Agex Ventures is a diversified infrastructure and industrial services enterprise headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan. We deliver high-performance solutions across two critical pillars of India’s growth: Civil Construction and Oil & Gas Services.

From landmark residential and commercial developments to complex industrial pipelines and oilfield support operations, Agex Ventures brings the technical depth, financial discipline, and operational agility demanded by large-scale projects across India.

We operate as a partner of choice for public sector undertakings, EPC contractors, private developers, and oil & gas majors—delivering projects on schedule, within budget, and to the highest quality benchmarks.

What We Deliver

Our Services

Agex Ventures operates across two high-impact verticals. Select a domain to explore our full service capabilities.

01

Residential & Commercial Construction

End-to-end construction of residential complexes, commercial towers, housing projects, and mixed-use developments. We handle design coordination, structural execution, finishing, and handover.

02

Road & Infrastructure Development

Construction and upgradation of highways, state roads, rural connectivity projects, flyovers, and allied infrastructure meeting NHAI, PMGSY, and state PWD standards.

03

Industrial Projects

Construction of factories, warehouses, processing units, and industrial parks for heavy-industry clients with strict adherence to industrial safety norms and operational requirements.

04

RCC & Structural Work

Reinforced cement concrete structures, pre-engineered building systems, steel fabrication, and structural retrofitting. Precision execution for greenfield and brownfield projects.

05

Site Development & Preparation

Comprehensive site grading, leveling, excavation, retaining wall construction, and infrastructure provisioning. We prepare sites to the highest technical standards so every project begins on solid ground.

01

Pipeline Installation & Maintenance

Design-to-commissioning pipeline services for crude oil, natural gas, and petrochemical transport. Certified teams handle trench preparation, pipe laying, welding, hydro-testing, and long-term maintenance.

02

Oil Field Support Services

Comprehensive support for upstream and midstream oil & gas fields including civil works at well sites, production facility construction, access road development, and camp infrastructure management.

03

Equipment Handling & Logistics

Specialized heavy equipment mobilization, rigging, and placement for oil & gas installations. Critical plant and machinery delivered safely, on schedule, and in full regulatory compliance.

04

Industrial Maintenance Services

Scheduled and emergency maintenance of process equipment, pipelines, storage tanks, and mechanical systems at refineries and production facilities—minimizing downtime and maximizing efficiency.
